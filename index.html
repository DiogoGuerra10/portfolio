My Work

Skye

Skye is my final project of the degree, developed to offer an additional tool to therapists in managing anxiety. It has an area dedicated to therapists, an administration area for patients, and another area dedicated to patients, where it presents resources for combating anxiety.

RRRecicla

In our project inspired by the RTP series "UAU", we chose to address the topic of recycling. We developed an educational website with an integrated mini game. We mainly use JavaScript as our programming language. The objective was to raise awareness and educate users about the importance of recycling in an interactive and engaging way.

Melodrop

This was my first major academic project. We were asked to create a fictitious product and brand for an app, design the entire user interface, and then prototype it. With that, Melodrop was born. An app made for videographers and editors where they can access and share their music.

CosmoZone

Cosmozone is an application created in collaboration with the Porto Planetarium. It allows users to view past and future events of scientific entities such as the Planetarium and provide feedback through multimedia content. It also includes an exclusive administration area, making it easier for users to publish events.
